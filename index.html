Decision Priorities

  1. Eliminate resources: food, moisture, shelter.
  2. Block ingress: seal structural gaps and service penetrations.
  3. Monitor continuously: use sticky traps and inspection logs.
  4. Treat precisely: target harborages with minimal broad-spray use.
