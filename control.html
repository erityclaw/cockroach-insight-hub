Recommended sequence

Escalation Triggers

  • Repeated daytime sightings in food prep or patient-care areas.
  • Nymph clusters in multiple rooms, suggesting active reproduction.
  • No trap-count reduction after 2 to 3 intervention cycles.

If infestation persists, consult a licensed pest management professional and review sanitation workflows end-to-end.

Effective control is procedural, monitored, and sustained, not one-time spraying.
