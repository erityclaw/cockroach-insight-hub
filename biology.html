Biology and Lifecycle Dynamics

Understanding anatomy and reproduction helps prioritize control timing and methods.

Anatomy

Flattened body form supports movement through narrow gaps. Antennae provide high chemical sensitivity for food and pheromone trails.

Behavior

Nocturnal activity dominates. Sudden daytime activity can signal crowding or resource depletion in harborages.

Reproduction

Egg case production rates vary by species and temperature. Warm, humid interiors accelerate population growth.
